Safety features of Tesla model 3

As mentioned in the official website of Tesla, Tesla Model 3 is one of the safest car of the world. It has High impect protection, rigid body structure and very low rollover risk.

Rigid Structure : The entire body is made of a material mix of aluminum and steel which provides best structural rigidity and maximum strength.

Impact Protection : Model 3 offers impect absorbing rails and center pillar rainforcement and the battery pack provide protection from every side.

Very Low Rollover Risk : The position and the weight of the whole car is Very well balanced. A very low center of gravity.

Over all Tesla Model 3 gets 5 star rating for it's safety.
