About Tesla Model 3

Tesla model 3 is an electric prowered car. It falls under the sedan family. They offers 4 doors in the car. The production of the car was started from july 2017.

The reason, it is so popular is because it is Fully Electric, hence Environment Friendly, Best Safety Features, Long Milage, Quickest Acceleration, All Wheel Drive, Auto Pilot feature and Modern Design.

Specifications:

  • Battery : designed for long range in one full charge.

  • Weight : 4,065 lbs (1844 Kg.).

  • Acceleration : Only takes 3.1 seconds to reach 0 to 60 mph (0 - 96 Kph).

  • Range : 315 miles (507 Km.) according to Tesla.

  • Seating Capacity : 5 Adults according to Tesla.

  • Drive : Dual Motor All-Wheel Drive.

  • Control Display : 15" Center Touchscreen.

  • Onboard Charger : 11.5 kW max (48A).

  • Supercharging : 250 kW max.
